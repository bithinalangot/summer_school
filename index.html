For Six days,
get an in-depth exposure into the area of Information Security

Information Security and Protection is a discipline that lies at the crossroads of security, cryptography, networks, software engineering, computer architecture, operating systems, and compiler design. The summer school will bring together internationally-­‐recognized experts to describe the advanced techniques that are used for protecting software from tampering, reverse engineering, and piracy
